Day 1: The Trip and Arrival

In 2013 my friend Kelly invited me and my buddy Justin to come vist her in her new home Fort Collins, Colorado.

I was intially not sure. Taking trips to random places wasn't my thing, but I ignored these intial hesitations and decided to make the journey out there with Justin in October of 2013

So we set up on our long journey...

Good Kid, MAAD City was our soundtrack

We dealt with the long boredom that is driving through the plains of Kansas for eight hours

Justin called me a 'beggin ass broke boy' because I had spare change to pay the toll

Finally we arrived in Fort Collins, after 13 plus hours of driving. Kelly took us to a bar where we had our first Fort Collins Beer...

An Odell 90 Schilling
